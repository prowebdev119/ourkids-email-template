Happy New Year from Our Kids Read!
We’ve received a number of e-mails from friends, donors and supporters asking what’s in store for Our Kids Read in 2023.
Well here it is! Here’s a look at what’s on deck for OKR for the New Year:
TECHNOLOGY
  • Earlier in December 2022, Our Kids Read OKR was accepted into the Microsoft Nonprofit Tech Acceleration (NTA) program which includes a $3,000 technology grant + 300+ free Office 365 licenses from Microsoft. This award will be used to kick-start the development of our Reading Buddies online platform (Microsoft is developing the portal through Benevity’s volunteer program and donating dollars to the organization for ever hour spent programming, thanks Brent Nardone!):
We received the first mock-up of this portal yesterday (portal will launch:
EXPANDING OUR READING BUDDIES PROGRAM
  • Reading Buddy pilots are kicking off at the Boys and Girls Club, Pinkerton Club House in Harlem, the Jasmine Nyree Community Center in Pittsburgh and 6 other locations in the first half of 2023 (special thanks to Jerome Bettis and the Get on the Bus Foundation for the introduction to the Jasmine Nyree team during our 12/17/22 Free Book Festival in Pittsburgh)
  • Thanks for a $6,000 grant from the Myers Park United Methodist Church (MPUMC). They applied for a grant from the Giles Foundation on our behalf and we got the good news last month! As a result, w were are able to hire a Spanish-speaking parent coordinator to increase the number of Spanish-speaking students from 4 to 26 in the January 2023 – April 2023 Reading Buddies session. Special thanks to John Clarkson, Princeton ’64 for the MPUMC introduction.
From left to right, OKR volunteer John Clarkson, Princeton Class of ‘64, Julie Wentz, Rev. Nathan Arledge
CAPACITY BUILDING
We’re hiring! We’ve expanded out team to include a Spanish-speaking parent-coordinator to ensure that the Latinx populations in the schools we serve get the service they deserve.
We’ve also hired a grant writer to help us reach more children in 2024 (we expect ro spend the next 12 months grant writing with the expectation that a percentage of the applications wil bear fruit in 2024).
What a blessing you all have been to the children we served in 2022!
Here’s to an even more impactful 2023.
Again … THANK YOU!
